What does BMI mean?

Body mass index (BMI) is a measurement which indicates how healthy your height and weight proportions are. It is calculated by dividing the weight (in kilograms) and height (in centimeters). This simple formula can be lifechanging and help you stay on track for a healthy and prosperous life!
